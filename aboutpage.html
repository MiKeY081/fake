OUR STORY

USAL's Background Story

How we started and why we're here

The Vision

USA Self Learn (USAL) was born out of a simple yet powerful vision: to make the dream of studying in the USA accessible to every student without the heavy price tag.

The Problem

For too long, aspiring students have been exploited by agencies that take advantage of their desperation, charging exorbitant fees for services that should be available for free.

The Solution

USAL was created to put an end to this injustice and provide the support that every student deserves — free, transparent, and reliable.

The Founder's Experience

The founder, having experienced the struggle firsthand, saw how many agencies were taking advantage of students, offering empty promises and charging high fees for basic guidance that could easily be found elsewhere.

The Growth

What started as a small Facebook and Telegram group quickly grew into a powerful community, providing thousands of students with the tools and knowledge they need to succeed — at no cost.

The Impact

In just 9 months, USAL has helped thousands of students take control of their futures and successfully navigate the complicated process of studying in the USA.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about studying in the USA

How is USAL different from paid agencies?

Unlike paid agencies that charge thousands of rupees for their services, USAL provides all information and guidance completely free of charge. We're a community-driven platform where students help each other navigate the process of studying in the USA. We believe that quality information should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial situation.

How do I join the USAL community?

Joining USAL is simple and free! You can join our Facebook group, Telegram group, or sign up for our email newsletter. Once you're a member, you'll have access to all our resources, guides, and can participate in community discussions.

What kind of resources does USAL provide?

USAL provides comprehensive resources on university selection, application processes, visa requirements, scholarship opportunities, standardized test preparation, and more. We also offer personalized guidance through our community forums and regular Q&A sessions with students who have successfully navigated the process.

How can I contribute to the USAL community?

There are many ways to contribute! You can share your own experiences and insights, help answer questions from other students, create or improve resources, or volunteer as a mentor. If you've successfully navigated the process of studying in the USA, your knowledge is invaluable to others who are just starting their journey.

Is USAL affiliated with any universities or agencies?

No, USAL is an independent, student-led community. We are not affiliated with any universities, agencies, or commercial entities. This independence allows us to provide unbiased information and guidance without any conflicts of interest.
