Step-by-Step Guide

USA Visa Process

Follow these essential steps to successfully navigate the US visa application process

Determine Visa Type

Identify the visa that fits your purpose of travel, such as F-1 for students or B-1/B-2 for business/tourism.

Complete DS-160 Form

Fill out the online non-immigrant visa application form with accurate personal and travel information.

Pay Visa Fee

Pay the non-refundable visa application fee online or at designated banks.

Schedule Appointments

Book biometric appointment for fingerprints and photo, and visa interview at the US Embassy or Consulate.

Prepare for Interview

Gather required documents and practice answering common interview questions confidently.
